My top three series of all time

Game of Thrones

The series takes place in the fictional continents of Westeros and Essos, where noble families vie for control of the Iron Throne, the seat of power in Westeros. Themes of political intrigue, betrayal, war, and morality are central to the plot. The show features multiple storylines, including the struggles of the Stark family from the North, the Lannisters in the South, and the rise of Daenerys Targaryen in the East, who seeks to reclaim the Iron Throne for her family.

Stranger Things

The series is set in the 1980s in the fictional town of Hawkins, Indiana. It begins with the mysterious disappearance of a young boy named Will Byers, and as his friends and family search for him, they uncover a secret government experiment and a parallel dimension called the Upside Down. The show blends supernatural horror, government conspiracies, and personal relationships, as well as a strong sense of nostalgia for the 1980s pop culture.

Peaky Blinders
